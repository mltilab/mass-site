Let's talk about your study

We're partnering with early research teams now. Tell us what you're working on and we'll set up a walkthrough on footage like yours.

What to expect

  • A real demo, not a slide deck — we'll walk the full pipeline: agent-guided setup, annotation in the labeler, and your data on the activity map
  • Honest fit assessment — if MASS isn't right for your study design, we'll say so
  • Early-partner input — our roadmap is shaped by the research teams using the platform; early partners get a direct line

Good questions to bring

  • What does your footage look like — classroom, lab, field?
  • What behaviors or activities do you code today, and how long does it take?
  • How many coders are on your team, and how do you handle agreement?
